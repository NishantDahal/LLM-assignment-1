About This Project

This web app demonstrates word embeddings using two methods:

  • GloVe: Global Vectors for Word Representation, a pre-trained model that captures semantic relationships between words.
  • TF-IDF: Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency, which weighs the importance of words within documents in a corpus.

Enter words to see their vector representations and find semantically similar words. The visualization uses dimensionality reduction techniques (t-SNE and PCA) to display the high-dimensional word vectors in 2D space.
